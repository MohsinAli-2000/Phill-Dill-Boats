Welcome to Phil Dill Marine

Welcome to Clearwater Marine in Clearwater, FL—your premier source for pre-owned boats! We’re committed to redefining the used boat buying experience with transparency and quality. Our meticulous vessel condition reports highlight each boat’s true value, while our reconditioning process ensures every boat is delivered with working electronics, a functional stereo, essential safety features, a freshly serviced engine, and a fully inspected trailer. At Clearwater Marine, trust, quality, and exceptional service come together to provide you with the best pre-owned boating experience.
